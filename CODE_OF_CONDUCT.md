# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, specific and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing and deliberately disruptive behavior are not accepted in issues, discussions, pull requests or other project spaces.

Assume good intent, criticize ideas and code rather than people, and remember that contributors may have different languages and levels of experience. Maintainers may edit, hide or remove content and restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Do not disclose sensitive personal information in a public issue.
